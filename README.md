# IntelliCold 🧊

> AI-Driven Cold Chain Spoilage Prediction and Smart Delivery Optimization System

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Existing cold chain systems only monitor temperature and react after damage occurs. They lack spoilage prediction, intelligent action recommendations, and quality-aware delivery decisions, leading to high losses of temperature-sensitive goods.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**IntelliCold** predicts spoilage 24-48 hours in advance using temperature-exposure based ML, provides continuous quality estimation, automatically triggers corrective actions (cooling control, route optimization), and prioritizes deliveries based on real-time spoilage risk.

---

## ⚡ Key Innovations

1. **Temperature-exposure based ML prediction** - Replaces expensive gas sensors
2. **Continuous quality estimation** - Real-time spoilage probability (0-100%), quality %, and Remaining Shelf Life (hours)
3. **Multi-level risk classification** - Low / Medium / High / Critical
4. **Autonomous cooling control** - Automatic Peltier module activation
5. **Cold chain transport integration** - Monitors during transit
6. **Product-aware management** - Different products, different thresholds
7. **Delivery priority queue** - High-risk shipments delivered first
8. **Low-cost hardware** - ESP32-based system 

---

## 🎯 Target Use Cases

- Food supply chain 
- Cold storage warehouses
